# Codebook 

summarise.txt: 
A tidy data set with the average of 66 variables for 6 activities and 30 subjects (180 groups).

1.activity
	the activity names
		WALKING

		WALKING_UPSTAIRS

		WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS

		SITTING

		STANDING
		
LAYING
